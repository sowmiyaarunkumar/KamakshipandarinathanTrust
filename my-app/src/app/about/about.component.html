About Us

Personal care for your healthy living

Kamakshi Pandarinathan Trust also stated as KPT is one of the renowned Non-Profit charitable organization pioneers in serving Persons and families with Disabilities. It is a single shelter that caters facilities for the people across their life span irrespective of their socio economic and geographic entities. The sole motive of KPT was to uplift the underprivilege persons in all the ways. The Trust was founded by Shri. P. Paulraj in 2004 and is effectively functioning by the charitable fund provided by a few goodwill individuals, organizations, fund raised against the home stay and schemes approved by National as well as State Government including Tamil Nadu Chief Minister’s Comprehensive Health Insurance Scheme from the year 2017.

Located at a rural backdrop and opposite to collectorate office, fulfils its dual purpose by finding it effortlessly distinguishable and conveniently accessible to the underprivileged and difficult to commute population. Trust is novel in it endeavors as it incorporates evidence based interdisciplinary approach in habilitation and rehabilitation of persons with disabilities. When the Managing trustee Shri. P. Sugumar an Occupational Therapist along with the Founder intended to empower the persons having disabilities. For the good cause to begin, founder has handed over his own building of 2400 sq. ft area, a Maruthi Omni as well to the trust. This aided in starting Saisri Paulson’s Multi-Specialty Therapy Centre, Srimathi Karthikeyani school for special Children and proving free transportation service to the families’ attending services at KPT. The therapy center and special school is now serving nearly 1000 children and adults with disabilities every month since 2004.

Mision

To be one of the topnotch organization fervent pertaining to Welfare and Rehabilitation of Persons and Children with Disabilities using Evidence Based Approach, Upliftment of Women & Children who are economically underprivileged and raise a one stop life care home through public care services.

Strive to provide a better quality of life for the persons in need of by imbibing the knowledge of inclusion, empowerment, self-advocacy and equal participation.

To work on quality, original and field-based research focusing on disabilities expertise & interventions.

To undertake and complete national and international projects pertaining to the field of education, health sciences, allied health sciences, humanities and social services within the stipulated time.

To work on to sculpt ethically virtuous human resource in the field of disability.

Vision

Our vision is to evolve as a state-of-art one stop rehabilitation center for all so as to provide diverse facilities regardless of their disabilities, socio – economic status and geographical entities.

Value Statement

Involving in the rehabilitation of every single individual by creating an example of classless society by being as an egalitarian organization. To preach and teach equal opportunities, protection of rights and full participation among persons with disabilities.

Objective

The main objectives of the trust are to deliver services to persons with disabilities and under-privileged organization.

Kamakshi Pandarinathan Trust fascinates people in need of rehabilitation all over southern districts of Tamil Nadu however, realized in extending its service at National level by involving in more collaborations at district, national and state level.

The Trust started with a modest multi-specialty clinic and now have successfully established itself in rendering services to toddlers & children with special needs, persons with psychological issues, geriatric people. Further portrayed to be the one stop center from infants to geriatrics by conducting more public awareness camps and community-based rehabilitation services.

The Trust attracted mostly the under privileged and women empowerment earlier now it encompasses nearly 10 disabilities included in Persons with Disability act, 2016. It will develop further to include all the 21 disabilities included in the Acts as well as the disabilities included in other Sate and Central Government Act like National Trust Act.

It predominantly focuses on habilitation and rehabilitation of Persons and their families with Disabilities. It will cater it services in detail pertaining to medical, allied health and humanitarian wings.

To participate and/ or undertake training programs. To train the trainees in area of rehabilitation and nursing pertaining to disabilities. To commence human resource development in field of disabilities.

To involve in state and central government scheme creating awareness and distribution camps for persons with disabilities.
